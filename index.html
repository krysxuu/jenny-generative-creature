Appearance:

Jenny, the magical bunny, is an adorable creature with a soft, rounded body and head that seamlessly blend into a smooth, gentle shape. Her long ears wiggle naturally, moving up and down in a rhythmic, playful motion as if she’s responding to the surrounding energy. Jenny’s large, curious eyes giving her a sense of life and attentiveness. What makes Jenny truly magical is her dynamic, color-changing appearance: as you move the mouse horizontally across the screen, her body transforms into a beautiful gradient of colors, shifting smoothly through shades of purple, pink, blue, and red, creating a mesmerizing, rainbow-like effect. This vibrant display brings Jenny to life, making her appear like a living, breathing part of an enchanted, ever-changing world..

Key Characteristics:

  • changes color
  • Feeds on glowing fruits
  • Jenny has long, pointed ears like a bunny, which it uses to listen to the faintest sounds of approaching predators or lost travelers

What we know so far about Jenny the Bunny? Jenny is a mystical guardian of the Twilight Forest, a magical and ever-changing environment filled with glowing plants and enchanted energy. Born from the soft glow of moonlight and forest magic, Jenny embodies curiosity and agility, combining the features of a rabbit's gentle nature with a fox's silent grace. Jenny moves effortlessly, blending into the twilight as her colors shimmer in response to the energy around her. As the viewer interacts, Jenny’s body transforms, shifting across soft gradients of purple, as if she absorbs the hues of the twilight sky. Her wiggling ears and wide, observant eyes give her a playful yet vigilant demeanor, watching over the forest and responding to disturbances with quiet grace

How do we interact with this creature?

- Move the mouse: Jenny will smoothly follow the mouse cursor.
- Move horizontally: Watch Jenny’s body change color dynamically across shades of purple.
- Move vertically: The background lightens or darkens, reflecting the forest's changing mood.
- Observe closely: Jenny’s ears wiggle rhythmically.
- Click the mouse: Sparkling particles or glowing effects can appear to highlight Jenny’s magical aura.
